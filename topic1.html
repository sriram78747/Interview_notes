Types of Networks

Here are more use-case examples and comparisons:


LAN (Local Area Network)

  • Used in homes, offices, schools

  • Example: Your home Wi-Fi with a few connected devices

WAN (Wide Area Network)

  • Connects LANs across long distances

  • Example: A multinational company connecting branches across countries

MAN (Metropolitan Area Network)

  • Connects users in a city or town

  • Often maintained by ISPs or government entities

PAN (Personal Area Network)

  • Very limited range, typically 10 meters

  • Example: Connecting your mobile phone to wireless earphones or smartwatch
