Network Devices

Additions and deeper functions:


Router

  • Manages traffic between your network and the internet

  • Can assign IP addresses using DHCP

  • May offer port forwarding, firewall settings, and QoS (Quality of Service)

Switch

  • Smarter than a hub; knows where to send data

  • Used in enterprise networks to connect many computers

Modem

  • Stands for modulator-demodulator

  • Converts signals between digital (used by your devices) and analog (used by phone lines)

Firewall

  • Can be hardware (external box) or software (like Windows Defender Firewall)

  • Blocks unauthorized access and filters malicious traffic
