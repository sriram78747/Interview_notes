Wired vs Wireless Networks

Wired Pros:

  • Stable connection

  • Faster data transmission

  • More secure (harder to intercept)

Wired Cons:

  • Cables can clutter

  • Less mobility

Wireless Pros:

  • Convenient for mobile devices

  • Easy to expand the network

Wireless Cons:

  • Susceptible to interference

  • Slower and less secure unless encrypted
